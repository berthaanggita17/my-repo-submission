What is Acne ?

Acne is a skin condition that occurs when your hair follicles become plugged with oil and dead skin cells (quoted from mayoclinic). Acne is usually caused by various kinds, such as hormones, blackheads or whiteheads, certain food, and others.

Acne Medication

Acne can be done in various ways, such as traditional or modern ways. However, traditional methods are rarely used, because there are many modern treatments from beauty clinics to treat acne. I treated acne through a skin specialist for 3 years. This can improve the condition of my facial skin from acne, various treatments can be done such as peeling, facial, dermapen, prp, etc.

However, acne can also be treated by using a variety of special acne skincare that can be purchased at supermarkets. So, don't worry about acne, because acne will definitely heal if treated properly.
